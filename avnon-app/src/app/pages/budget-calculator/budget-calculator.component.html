Budget Calculator

Plan and manage your finances with our intuitive budget calculator tool

How to use:

  1. Select your desired date range using the start and end month pickers
  2. The calculator will automatically process your budget for the selected period
  3. Add custom rows using the "+" buttons
  4. Add custom categories with the "New Income Category" or "New Expense Category" buttons
  5. Right-click on a cell to access the context menu
  6. View calculated totals at the bottom of the table

This budget calculator helps you plan your expenses and income over custom time periods. Export your data anytime for further analysis.
